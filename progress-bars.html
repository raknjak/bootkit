PROGRESS BARS

Adding various styles to control the height of the progress bar.

Available classes

  • bk-progress-xxs: sets the height of the progress bar to 2 pixels.
  • bk-progress-xs: sets the height of the progress bar to 4 pixels.
  • bk-progress-sm: sets the height of the progress bar to 6 pixels.
  • bk-progress-md: sets the height of the progress bar to 8 pixels.
  • bk-progress-xl: sets the height of the progress bar to 10 pixels.
  • bk-progress-xxl: sets the height of the progress bar to 12 pixels.
  • bk-progress-xxxl: sets the height of the progress bar to 14 pixels.
Notes:
- box shadow on the inner progress bar is disabled. Should you need it comment out LINE 1-3 in bootkit/progress-bars.less.
- radius is turned off with bk-rd-off; this depends on personal taste and is not obligated.
