POINTERS

Triangular shapes which can be used as horizontal or vertical pointers aimed at another cell or panel body.
Default pointer height and width are 12 pixels but this can easily be changed with 2 LESS variables.

Available classes

  • bk-pt: declares a cell to havae a pointer.
  • bk-pt-l-*: adds a pointer to the left of a cell (replace the * by a color name e.g. success, danger, ...).
  • bk-pt-r-*: adds a pointer to the right of a cell.
  • bk-pt-t-*: adds a pointer to the top of a cell.
  • bk-pt-b-*: adds a pointer to the bottom of a cell.
